What is Voter Suppression?

According to Wikipedia, Voter suppression in the United States concerns allegations about various efforts, legal and illegal, used to prevent eligible voters from exercising their right to vote.

Who does it affect?

People of color, women, students, disabled, and low-income voters to name a few.
