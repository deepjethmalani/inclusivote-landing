MISSION

--------

A non-partisan immigrant-led initiative
to inform people about minority voter suppression and increase participation.



*We are a non-profit organization and do not endorse any candidate, elected official, or political party.

Message from the Founder

---------------

Blatant voter suppression is on the rise. It is unfair, unconstitutional, and discriminatory. Such acts disenfranchise racial minorities, communities of color, and immigrants alike.

Although a good number of us cannot actively participate in the US Elections due to several reasons, it is critical to mention that the outcome affects us all. As a fellow immigrant, I witness how it affects us daily. Therefore, those who cannot vote must urge others to go out and vote. To the ones who can vote, please go vote!

Voter suppression has an impact on all of us. We must educate so we may empower; join us in this fight against voter suppression.

1. Know your voting rights
2. Create your vote squad
3. Spread awareness by educating at least two people among your friends and/or family.

Finally, exercise your right to vote!

Sincerely,
Deep Jethmalani
